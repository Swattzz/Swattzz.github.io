About

I am a researcher working on near-zero-power IoT systems, focusing on ultra-low-power wireless communication under μW-level energy constraints. My work spans system architecture, communication protocols, and chip-level implementation for near-zero-power IoT.

I received my Ph.D. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohua Tian.

Research Interests

  • Near-zero-power wireless communication
  • Ultra-low-power IoT systems (<10μW)
  • Backscatter communication
  • Cross-layer system design
