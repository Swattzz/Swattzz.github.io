Fengyuan Zhu

Hi, I am a second-year Ph.D. student in EE department, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), Shanghai, China.

Here is my e-mail address in SJTU: jsqdzhufengyuan@sjtu.edu.cn

I received my bachelor's degree from EE department, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019. After that, I was admitted to the graduate school of Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I joined Research Center of Intelligent Internet of Things (IIoT) in 2017 and have been supervised by Professor Xiaohua Tian since then. My research work in IIoT includes backscatter communication. My research interests are mainly on wireless communication and various low-power designs for IoT.
