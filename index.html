Fengyuan Zhu

Hi, I am a first-year EE master student in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China.

I received my bachelor's degree from EE department, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019. After that, I was admitted to the graduate school of Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I joined Research Center of Intelligent Internet of Things (IIoT) in 2017 and have been supervised by Professor Xiaohua Tian and Xinbing Wang since then. My research work in IIoT includes backscatter communication and localization. My research interests are mainly on wireless communication and wireless sensing. Before joing IIoT, I also worked with with Professor Xiaohong Sui and Xiaohua Zhang in the area of biomedical engineering.

My Academic Experience

From 2015 to 2017

I worked with Jingran Zhu and Yiling Wang under the supervision of Prof. Sui in the department of Biomedical Engineering. The research topic is CNT electrodes.

From 2017 to 2020

In 2017, I joined IIoT and began to work with Renjie Zhao under the supervision of Prof. Tian. The research topic is OFDMA backscatter.

In 2018, I collabrated with Xinyu Tong and applied AoA localization technique to backscatter networks.

In 2019, I came up with an idea and began to build a more practical and large-scale backscatter network with Yuda Feng and Qianru Li. Meanwhile, Professor Tian became my M.S. advisor.

In 2020, I am still working on wireless communication. My teammates include Ke Li, Mingwei Ouyang, Zitong Wang, Zixuan Zhang and Zhexuan Cai.
