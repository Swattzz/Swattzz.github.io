NEWS

I am expected to receive my Ph.D. degree in September 2025 and will join Shanghai Jiao Tong University as a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Computer Science. My supervisor will be Prof. Xiaohua Tian.

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the EE department at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), Shanghai, China. I am expected to graduate and receive my Ph.D. degree in September 2025.

I received my bachelor's degree from the EE department, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019. After that, I was admitted to the graduate school of Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I am pursuing my Ph.D. degree in the EE department at Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Professor Xiaohua Tian.

My research interests include backscatter communication and various low-power IoT designs. My research works focus on developing next-generation IoT networks, with each IoT node consuming μW-level ultra-low power, without battery or life-span powered. My research works are published in top journals in networking: IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking (TON), IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing (TMC), and top conferences in mobile computing: ACM MobiCom, ACM MobiSys, ACM SenSys, and top conferences in microelectronics: International Solid-State Circuits Conference (ISSCC).

My Academic Experience

In 2017, I joined IIoT and began to work with Renjie Zhao under the supervision of Prof. Tian. The research topic is OFDMA backscatter.

In 2019, I began my M.S. program in the EE department at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My supervisor is Prof. Tian.

In 2021, I began my Ph.D. program in the EE department at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Tian.
