Fengyuan Zhu

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the EE department, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), Shanghai, China.

Here is my e-mail address in SJTU: jsqdzhufengyuan@sjtu.edu.cn

I received my bachelor's degree from the EE department, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019. After that, I was admitted to the graduate school of Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I was admitted to the Master Program in 2019 and transferred to the Ph.D. program in 2021 in the EE department, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My supervisor has been Professor Xiaohua Tian.

My research interests include wireless systems and various low-power IoT designs. My research works focus on developing next-generation IoT networks, with each IoT node consuming μW-level ultra-low power. My research works are published in top journals in networking: IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking (TON), IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing (TMC), and top conferences in mobile computing: ACM MobiCom, ACM MobiSys, ACM SenSys, and top conferences in microelectronics: International Solid-State Circuits Conference (ISSCC).

My Academic Experience

From 2017 to 2022

In 2021, I began my Ph.D. program in the EE department at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Tian.
